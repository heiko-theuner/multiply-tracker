Help
What is Multiply Tracker?

Multiply Tracker is a free app designed to help users improve multiplication skills. Customize learning, track progress, and support development through donations.

Why is Multiply Tracker free?

Multiply Tracker is ad-free and subscription-free. It's our commitment to offer a high-quality learning experience without any financial barriers.

How can I support the developer?

If you love Multiply Tracker, you can make a small donation through in-app purchases. Your contribution helps maintain the app's quality and keeps it free for all users.

Is my data safe?

Your privacy is a priority. Tracking is off by default, and we don't collect personal data without your consent. Your information is secure.

Can I customize my learning experience?

Absolutely! Multiply Tracker lets you tailor your training to your preferences, whether you prefer text or audio-only tasks.

Are there any ads or subscriptions?

No, Multiply Tracker is completely ad-free and doesn't require any subscriptions. We're dedicated to providing an uninterrupted learning environment.
